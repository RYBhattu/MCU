Deadpool and Wolverine

Who is Deadpool?

Deadpool is a fictional superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. Created by writer Fabian Nicieza and artist Rob Liefeld, the character first appeared in "The New Mutants" #98 in 1991.


Who is Wolverine?

Wolverine is a fictional superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. Created by writer Len Wein and artist Herb Trimpe, the character first appeared in "The Incredible Hulk" #180 in 1974.
